Welcome!!!

Welcome to St. Alphonsus RC Primary School, a place where discovery and education blend in a caring atmosphere. Our focus is on achieving high academic standards while shaping strong characters, nurturing a passion for learning that endures forever. At our school, we aim to inspire curiosity, encourage growth, and instill values that shape individuals for life.

A Beautiful short description of the history of the school:

Established in 1878, St. Alphonsus School stands as a beacon of educational excellence rooted in a rich legacy of over a century. Nestled in the heart of a picturesque town, the school began with a humble vision to provide quality education to the youth, fostering a nurturing environment that cherished both academic and moral growth. Initially founded by a group of devoted educators and local philanthropists, the school started in a modest building, gradually expanding its premises to accommodate a growing number of eager young minds.
